Privacy Policy for Cardly+

Effective Date: December 10, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Cardly+, the app designed to help you optimize your credit card rewards. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard your data.

Data Collection

We collect the following types of information to provide you with a personalized and seamless experience:

  • Personal Data: Name, email, device information, and preferences.
  • Card Information: Credit card details (encrypted), transaction history, and card usage patterns.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, search queries, and feedback.
  • Location Data: Your location to provide relevant recommendations for card usage.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and browser details.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Personalize your experience and provide card recommendations tailored to your preferences.
  • Process transactions and rewards efficiently.
  • Send you notifications and updates about your rewards status, available offers, and promotions.
  • Improve the app's performance and optimize user experience.
  • Ensure the security of your data and maintain the integrity of the app.

Important: We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We take the protection of your data seriously and employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information. These include:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data, including card details, is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored in secure cloud servers, protected by firewalls and access controls.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits to ensure the integrity of our systems.
  • Biometric Authentication: For added security, we support biometric authentication on supported devices.

Your Rights & Controls

As a user of Cardly+, you have the following rights with respect to your data:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can update or correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Portability: You can request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications and personalized notifications.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@cardlyplus.com.

Cookies & Tracking

Cardly+ uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. These technologies allow us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve app performance.
  • Provide targeted advertising and offers.

You can control cookie settings through your device settings or within the app.

Third-Party Services

Cardly+ may use third-party services to enhance functionality. These services include:

  • Payment Processors: We use secure payment gateways to process card transactions.
  • Analytics Providers: We use third-party analytics services to help us understand how users interact with the app.
  • Cloud Storage Providers: We store your data securely with cloud service providers.

Please note that these third-party providers have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised policy on this page with an updated effective date. We recommend that you review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
